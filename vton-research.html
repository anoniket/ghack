07 / Next Steps
Implementation Strategy
A phased plan to go from broken to bulletproof.
Phase 1 — Immediate Fix (Today)

Switch default back to Gemini 2.5 Flash Image

Change IMAGE_GEN from gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview to gemini-2.5-flash-image. This is a one-line change. NB1 is GA, stable, fast (1-4s), and proven to work in your Feb 14 build. Keep Pro as retry fallback.

Phase 2 — Add Fallback Chain (This Week)

NB1 → Pro → FASHN.ai

If NB1 fails (503/timeout), try Pro. If Pro fails, fall back to FASHN.ai API ($0.075/img). This gives you 3 independent providers. No single point of failure. Add a /health endpoint that checks model availability.

Phase 3 — Dedicated VTON (Next Sprint)

Integrate Vertex AI VTO or FASHN as primary

Test Google's virtual-try-on-001 — it's purpose-built, GA, and in your GCP ecosystem. Or go with FASHN v1.6 for best commercial VTON quality. Move Gemini to fallback/cheap tier.

Phase 4 — Premium Tier (Future)

FLUX.2 Pro for highest quality

For users who want best-possible results, offer a "HD" mode powered by FLUX.2 Pro ($0.03/MP). Best character consistency available. Up to 9 reference images. Position as a premium feature.
